Welcome to HUGODECA viewer

The HUGODECA viewer is a database for the visualization, mining and comparison of published omics data sets for the reproductive science community.

It was developed to assist scientists in the mining of a wide range of ultra-high-throughput reprogenomics data, including RNA and DNA sequencing data.
